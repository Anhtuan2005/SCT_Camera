# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Operators and developers monitoring local security cameras from a desktop dashboard. They need to verify live feeds, inspect detections, configure zones and lines, and review alerts without leaving the monitoring workflow.

## Product Purpose

SCT Camera turns webcam, video, and RTSP sources into a realtime monitoring dashboard with detection, tracking, behavior analysis, and alert delivery. Success means camera state and incidents are understandable at a glance, while detailed controls remain easy to reach.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, operational. The interface should feel dependable during long monitoring sessions and direct during incident review.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-style layouts, decorative dashboards, oversized metrics, novelty controls, and visual effects that compete with the camera feeds.

## Design Principles

1. Keep live video and camera health as the primary signals.
2. Make operational state explicit and easy to scan.
3. Keep common monitoring actions close to the content they affect.
4. Prefer familiar controls and predictable navigation.
5. Reveal advanced configuration progressively.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and keyboard access. Never communicate camera or alert state through color alone, and respect reduced-motion preferences.
